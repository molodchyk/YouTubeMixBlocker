# Privacy Policy

YouTube Mix Blocker does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data or browsing data.

The extension runs only on YouTube pages. It detects and removes YouTube Mix recommendations and cleans YouTube Mix watch URLs locally in the user's browser.

The extension stores local settings and counters in the browser, such as whether the badge counter is enabled and how many Mix cards or Mix URLs were handled. This data stays on the user's device and is used only to provide the extension's features.

YouTube Mix Blocker does not use analytics, tracking, remote code, or external servers.
